How to Check if a Multi-buy Deal is Actually Cheaper

A multi-buy deal is only useful if the price per item or per unit is lower than buying normally.

Quick answer

Divide the total deal price by the number of items, then compare it with the normal single item price.

What to check

  • Price per item.
  • Price per 100g, 100ml or unit.
  • Whether you will actually use all the items.
  • Whether a different brand is still cheaper.
