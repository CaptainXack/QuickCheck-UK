About QuickCheck UK

QuickCheck UK is a free collection of everyday calculators and checkers for UK households. It is built to help with quick estimates for bills, budgets, rent, travel, work pay, shopping, family costs, DIY, tech and general planning.

Built for real-life rough figures

Many tools use simple choices like low, average, high, small, medium and large. That means visitors can still get a useful estimate even when they do not know the exact number.

What the site does

QuickCheck UK is designed for fast everyday checks.

Important note about estimates

The calculators are planning tools only. Results are rough estimates and should not be treated as financial, legal, tax, housing, medical, energy or professional advice.

For decisions involving contracts, debt, benefits, tax, rent, mortgages, insurance, energy tariffs or legal rights, always check official documents or speak to the relevant provider or qualified adviser.

Why it exists

The aim is simple: make everyday numbers easier to understand without requiring signups, paid accounts, downloads or a spreadsheet wrestling match.
