Electricity Running Cost Calculator

Use this to estimate how much an appliance costs to run based on watts, hours used and your electricity unit rate.

Example

A 2,000W heater used for 2 hours uses around 4kWh. Multiply that by your unit rate to estimate the cost.

Rough estimates only. Real costs can vary with thermostats, settings and tariff changes.

What the result means

The result is an estimate of cost over time. It helps compare appliances or spot which items may be adding most to your bill.
