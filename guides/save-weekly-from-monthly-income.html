How to Save Weekly From Monthly Income

A weekly saving target can make monthly income easier to manage. The safest amount is based on what is left after bills and essentials.

Quick method

Work out monthly leftover, choose a savings target, then divide the monthly saving amount by 4.33.

Simple plan

  • Calculate monthly income after tax or regular payments.
  • Subtract bills, food, travel and debt payments.
  • Choose a small amount that still leaves breathing room.
  • Move the weekly amount into savings after income arrives.
  • Review it when bills or income change.
