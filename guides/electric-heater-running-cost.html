How Much Does an Electric Heater Cost to Run?

The running cost depends on the heater wattage, how long it is on, and the electricity unit rate.

Quick answer

Cost equals kilowatts multiplied by hours used, then multiplied by your electricity unit price.

What affects the cost?

  • A 2kW heater uses around 2 units of electricity per hour at full power.
  • Thermostats may reduce use once the room is warm.
  • Short bursts cost less than leaving it on for hours.
