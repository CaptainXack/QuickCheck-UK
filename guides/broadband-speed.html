Broadband speed explained

Broadband speed needs depend on how many people use the connection at the same time and what they are doing.

  • Browsing and email need little speed.
  • HD and 4K streaming need more headroom.
  • Gaming needs stable latency as well as speed.
  • Home working needs reliable upload and video call performance.

The broadband checker gives a rough starting point.
