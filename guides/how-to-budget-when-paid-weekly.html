How to Budget When Paid Weekly

Weekly pay can feel easier because money arrives often, but monthly bills can still catch you out. The trick is to turn monthly bills into weekly set-aside amounts.

Quick method

Add regular monthly bills, multiply by 12, then divide by 52. That gives the weekly amount to put aside before spending.

Step by step

  • Write down your weekly pay after deductions.
  • List monthly bills such as rent, council tax, energy, phone and subscriptions.
  • Convert monthly bills to weekly using monthly × 12 ÷ 52.
  • Set that amount aside each week before food, travel and extras.
  • Track bill due dates so a heavy week does not surprise you.
