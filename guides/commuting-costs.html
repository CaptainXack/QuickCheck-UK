How Much Does Commuting Really Cost?

Commuting costs can include fuel, bus or train fares, parking, extra childcare time and wear on the car.

Quick answer

Work out the daily cost, multiply by working days, then compare it with the pay you keep after travel.

Costs to include

  • Fuel, parking or public transport.
  • Number of work days per week.
  • Extra time spent travelling.
  • Any extra childcare or food costs caused by the commute.
