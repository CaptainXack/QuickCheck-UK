Budget Planner vs Bills Calculator

Use this to check whether your income still leaves enough for rent, bills, food, travel, savings and everyday spending.

Example

Income: £1,800/month · Rent: £650/month · Bills: £260/month · Food and travel: £390/month. The calculator helps estimate what is left.

Rough estimates only. This is for planning, not financial advice.

What the result means

A positive leftover amount means your planned costs fit inside your income. A low or negative amount means you may need to adjust bills, rent, spending or savings goals.
