SIM Only vs Phone Contract: Which is Cheaper?

To compare properly, look at the total cost over the full contract, not just the monthly payment.

Quick answer

Add the upfront cost and every monthly payment, then compare that total with buying the phone separately plus a SIM only plan.

What to compare

  • Upfront phone cost.
  • Monthly contract cost.
  • Length of contract.
  • SIM only monthly cost.
  • Any price rises during the contract.
