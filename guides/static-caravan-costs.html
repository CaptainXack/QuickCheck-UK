Static caravan ownership cost guide

Static caravan ownership can include site fees, insurance, utilities, maintenance, decking, gas checks, transport, pitch rules and resale conditions.

  • Annual site fees
  • Insurance
  • Gas and electric
  • Water and waste
  • Repairs and cleaning
  • Commission if sold through the site
