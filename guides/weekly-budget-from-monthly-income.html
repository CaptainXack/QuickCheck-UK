How to Work Out a Weekly Budget From Monthly Income

A weekly budget turns a monthly income amount into something easier to manage day to day.

Quick answer

Add monthly income, subtract regular bills and essentials, then divide what is left by the number of weeks until the next payday.

Simple method

  • Write down monthly income after tax.
  • Take off rent, council tax, energy, broadband and phone.
  • Take off food, travel, school costs and regular essentials.
  • Divide the remaining amount by 4.33 for an average weekly figure.
