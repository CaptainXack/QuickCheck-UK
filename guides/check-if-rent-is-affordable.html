How to Check if Rent is Affordable

Rent affordability is not just the rent amount. You need to include bills, food, travel and other fixed costs.

Quick answer

Compare rent against take-home income, then check what remains after bills and essentials.

Things to include

  • Rent and deposit.
  • Council tax and energy bills.
  • Broadband, phone and insurance.
  • Food, travel and school costs.
