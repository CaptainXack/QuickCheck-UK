🔒 Privacy and data

Privacy Policy

QuickCheck UK is designed to be used without creating an account. The calculators are intended to run in your browser and provide rough estimates from the values you enter.

Calculator values

Calculator values are used to produce on-page results. Do not enter private or sensitive information that you would not want visible on your own screen.

Information you choose to send

If you contact QuickCheck UK by email, your email address and message contents will be visible to the mailbox receiving your message so the site owner can respond.

Cookies, advertising and analytics

This site may use third-party advertising or analytics services. Those services may use cookies, device information or similar technologies according to their own policies.

If Google AdSense or another advertising provider is enabled, adverts should remain clearly separate from calculator tools and content.

Local browser features

Some browser features such as install prompts, copied results, print actions or saved preferences may depend on your own device and browser.

External links

QuickCheck UK may link to external websites. External sites have their own privacy policies and are not controlled by QuickCheck UK.
