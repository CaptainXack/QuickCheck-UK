How to Reduce Subscription Costs

Small subscriptions can quietly become a large monthly outgoing. Listing them together makes it easier to decide what to keep, pause or cancel.

Quick method

Add every repeat payment, check the monthly and yearly total, then compare that total with your monthly leftover.

What to check

  • Streaming and music services.
  • Cloud storage, apps and games.
  • Phone plans and add-ons.
  • Gym, delivery and membership payments.
  • Free trials that became paid subscriptions.
