How to Work Out Money Left After Bills

Money left after bills is your income minus regular outgoings. It is the starting point for safe spending, saving and planning.

Quick method

Monthly income minus monthly bills and essentials equals monthly leftover. Divide by 4.33 for an average weekly figure.

Include these costs

  • Housing and council tax.
  • Energy, water, broadband and phone.
  • Food, travel and family costs.
  • Debt payments and subscriptions.
  • Any money you want to save every month.
