How Much Does School Uniform Cost?

Uniform costs can build up quickly when you include shoes, PE kit, bags, coats and replacements through the year.

Quick answer

List every item needed, add a replacement allowance, then divide the total by the months until you need to buy it.

Items to include

  • Logo items and basic uniform.
  • Shoes, PE kit and school bag.
  • Coat, lunch items and stationery.
  • Replacement items during the year.
