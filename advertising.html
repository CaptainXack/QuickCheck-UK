📢 Advertising information

Advertising on QuickCheck UK

QuickCheck UK may display advertising to help keep the calculator tools free to use.

Clear separation

Advertising should remain clearly separated from calculator tools, results and editorial content.

Advertising standards

QuickCheck UK should not ask users to click adverts, refresh pages, or interact with adverts to support the site. Visitors should use the tools normally.

Ad providers

If third-party advertising providers such as Google AdSense are used, those providers may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies.

Advertiser contact

For advertising or partnership enquiries, email simonjhackett.sh1@outlook.com.

Editorial independence

Calculator results should not be changed to favour an advertiser. Results should remain useful, clear and estimate-based.
