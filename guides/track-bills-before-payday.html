How to Track Bills Before Payday

Bills before payday are the ones most likely to cause stress. Tracking the date and amount lets you see what needs covering before new income arrives.

Quick method

Add each bill with its next due date, then total the bills due before your next payday.

What to track

  • Rent, mortgage or board.
  • Council tax, energy and water.
  • Phone, broadband and subscriptions.
  • Debt payments and insurance.
  • Any weekly or four-weekly payments.
