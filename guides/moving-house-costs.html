Moving house cost checklist

Moving costs are often more than the deposit and first rent. Build a buffer before agreeing to a move.

  • Deposit
  • First rent or mortgage payment
  • Van or removals
  • New furniture or appliances
  • Broadband setup
  • Travel during the move
  • Cleaning and small repairs

Use the moving cost calculator on the homepage to total the basics.
