AdSense setup notes

Keep ads clearly separated from calculator buttons and results. Do not ask users to click ads, refresh pages or interact with adverts.

  • Use ad spaces between sections.
  • Keep the tool usable without blocking overlays.
  • Add privacy details before applying.
  • Replace the placeholder ads.txt line after approval.

Good ad sites are useful first. Ads should sit around the value, not replace it.
