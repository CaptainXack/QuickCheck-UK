How to Budget When Paid Monthly

Monthly pay can disappear quickly if bills and weekly spending are not separated. A simple monthly budget gives every pound a job before payday excitement takes over.

Quick method

Take monthly income, subtract regular bills and essentials, then divide the leftover by the weeks until next payday.

Step by step

  • Start with take-home income for the month.
  • Subtract rent, council tax, energy, water, phone and subscriptions.
  • Subtract food, travel, family costs and debt payments.
  • Keep some money aside for irregular costs.
  • Divide what remains into weekly spending pots.
